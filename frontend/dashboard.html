Feature Guide

🔥

Streak Counter

Your streak tracks your daily commitment. If you complete at least one task on a given day, your streak increases by 1. If you complete zero tasks on a day, your streak resets back to 0. It doesn't matter how many tasks you finish — just one is enough to keep the streak alive and growing.

📊

Progress Zone (Pie Chart)

The pie chart in the left panel shows a live breakdown of your completed vs pending tasks. The percentage updates every time you complete or delete a task. Use it to get a quick snapshot of how much work is done and how much remains.

🚨

Deadline Alert Card

When a task's deadline is today or already overdue, a red alert card appears at the top of your task list. This is your urgent attention zone. Tasks in this card pulse red to grab your focus. Complete them and upload proof to stop the reminders.

📸

Proof of Completion

For tasks where you selected "Upload photo as proof", you must upload an image to mark the task complete. This makes completion verifiable — you can't just click done without evidence. Once uploaded, email reminders for that task stop immediately.

📥

Export Tasks

Found in the Profile menu (top right avatar). Downloads all your current tasks as a CSV file — a spreadsheet you can open in Excel or Google Sheets. Each row shows the task name, deadline, and whether it is completed or pending. Useful if you want to keep a record of your work, share your task list with a teacher or manager, or back up your tasks.

⚙️

Settings & Preferences

Access the Settings from your profile dropdown to customize your experience. You can configure notification preferences, theme appearance, task management options, and security settings. Changes are automatically saved and applied across your session.

🔔

Notification System

Email notifications are sent to remind you of upcoming deadlines. You can customize reminder times in Settings. Notifications stop automatically when a task is completed with proof. Make sure your email address is correct in your profile.

🎨

Theme & Display

Switch between Light and Dark themes using the theme toggle in the navigation bar. Your preference is saved automatically. The interface adapts to provide optimal visibility in different lighting conditions.

📱

Mobile & Responsive

DeadlineShield works seamlessly on mobile devices. The interface automatically adjusts to provide the best experience on phones, tablets, and desktop computers. All features are fully functional across devices.

🔒

Privacy & Security

Your data is securely stored and protected. Sessions automatically timeout for security. You can configure additional security options in Settings. We never share your personal information with third parties.

💡

Tips & Best Practices

• Set realistic deadlines to stay motivated
• Upload proof immediately after completing tasks
• Check daily to maintain your streak
• Export regularly for backup purposes
• Keep profile updated for proper notifications
